    How to Reduce Fabric Wastage

    • Plan your layout before cutting — industry standard wastage is 10–15%, efficient studios hit 5–8%
    • Group cuts of similar shapes to fill gaps; use offcuts for accessories or test stitching
    • Order fabric with a 6% buffer rather than 15% — the difference adds up fast
    • Cutting on the bias wastes more — only do it when the design demands it

    Track Time Accurately — Most Creative Workers Are Pricing Incorrectly

    • Batch production reduces per-unit time — making 5 at once is faster than 5 × 1
